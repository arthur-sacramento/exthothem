Our Challenge

Our challenge is to build a platform that follows the following principles:

  1. No one has a fixed salary. Their earnings are proportional to their productivity and the state of the market and platform growth.

  2. No one has an initial salary regardless of their position or previous experience. The quality and quantity of daily work will determine their hierarchy, position, values, and earnings.

  3. Anyone can participate in the project regardless of previous experience. They will learn what they need through the project itself.

  4. Anyone is free to work as many hours as they want, on any day and at any time.

Exthothem represents a new dawn in cryptocurrency development, setting itself apart with a unique blend of security, efficiency, and innovation. Our goal is to put into practice and develop a platform following these principles. Additionally, we aim to create an effective way of measuring the capabilities and skills of a programmer, serving as a portfolio to market their services to large companies or as freelancers.

Key Language Functions and Native Libraries

Hashing Functions: PHP offers a plethora of hashing functions like hash(), md5(), and sha256(). These functions are indispensable for creating secure hashes of transaction data, enhancing the integrity and security of Exthothem's blockchain.

Cryptography Libraries: The PHP OpenSSL extension equips Exthothem with powerful cryptographic functionalities. From digital signatures to encryption, OpenSSL enhances the security of transactions, safeguarding user assets.

File Handling: Efficient file handling is crucial for cryptocurrency data management. PHP's file functions, such as file_get_contents() and file_put_contents(), are invaluable for reading and storing blockchain data and user wallet information.

JSON Handling: Given JSON's ubiquity for data exchange, PHP's JSON functions are pivotal in serializing and deserializing data. These functions ensure seamless communication between Exthothem's components and external services.

Math Functions: Cryptocurrencies involve intricate mathematical calculations for consensus mechanisms and cryptographic operations. PHP's math functions, including gmp_add() and bcmath, support complex arithmetic operations vital for Exthothem's functionality.
